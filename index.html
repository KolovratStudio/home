About Us

Our Vision

Kolovrat Studio was born from the ancient forests and forgotten legends of Slavic lands. We are a team of two storytellers, artists, and craftsmen dedicated to bringing the rich tapestry of Slavic mythology to life through immersive gaming experiences.

Our mission is to create a game that resonates with the primal forces of nature, the mysteries of old gods, and the timeless struggle between light and darkness. Each title we craft is a journey into the unknown, a whisper from the past made tangible in the digital realm.

More >

Our Philosophy

We believe in quality over quantity, in depth over breadth. Every pixel, every line of code, every note of music is imbued with purpose and meaning. Our game is not a mere entertainment - it is a modern artifact, digital echoe of ancient wisdom.

Drawing inspiration from medieval manuscripts, folk tales, and archaeological discoveries, we strive to create experience that feels both authentic and innovative. The past is not dead - it merely sleeps, waiting to be awakened.

Our Game

Project "Kolovrat"

Journey through the forsaken lands of South Slavic myth in this dark fantasy RPG. As a wanderer between worlds, you must survive a decaying realm where ancient gods have fallen silent and monstrous spirits roam.

Project Kolovrat blends atmospheric pixel art, tactical combat, and meaningful choices into a brutal, haunting experience shaped by forgotten legends.

Deep Lore
